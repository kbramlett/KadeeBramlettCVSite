Web Development

Web Development is my newest creative outlet.

Certification Projects

During the most intense isolation phase of the COVID pandemic I earned the Responsive Web Development Certification on freeCodeCamp.org

To earn the certificate, I built several projects to pass automated test suites. I built a Portfolio Page, Survey Form, a Tribute Page, a Technical Documentation Page, and a Product Landing Page.

These projects have a lot of sentimental value for me now, as I remember how excited I was to learn and implement my new-found web development knowledge for the first time.

Copywriting - Amphibious Zoo

I never dreamed I'd get a gig that combined my musical background with my love of writing, but it happened!

I was asked to write descriptions for music tracks that are listed in a commercial music catalog. This catalog is where music supervisors on movies, TV, and commercials choose the music that will be included in sountracks.

My descriptions are the "sales pitch" to the music supervisors to convince them to click the track sample and potentially buy the music.

I wrote track descriptions for a music producer who lists his work in the Amphibious Zoo catalog, a commercial soundtrack company.

Read my track descriptions for Against the Clock, Last One Standing, and Swagger Trap - Volume 2

Visual Art

KMB Painted Glass

After a career as a classical musician and music educator, I discovered my love for artistic expression in visual art.

One of my priorities is to make beautiful things while minimizing the amount of waste in the process, so I make art out of reclaimed and upcycled objects.

The majority of my work is translucent paintings on glass in upcycled picture frames, most of which I find at thrift stores.

Original Music: Quantum Calm

In 2009, after I finished my Master of Music degree, I felt the need to be musically creative outside of the contsraints of Classical music. So, I started a project I named Quantum Calm.

I have composed and arranged all the music for this project.

This project started as a New Age duo with a guitarist and me playing oboe, piano, singing bowls, Native flutes, bells, etc.

We performed live for meditation groups, yoga classes, art shows, bookstores, and other similar settings.

The project later morphed into a wider range of timbres and textures produced with electronic sounds and digital techniques.

These are the albums that have been released under the Quantum Calm project:

  • Modus MIDI, 2022
  • Chill Textures, 2020
  • Hopeful Musings, 2020
  • Inhale, Exhale, 2011
